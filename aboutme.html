About Me

Graham Gammon-Everitt

Coder and Multidisciplined Artist

Good day to you! My name is Graham. I live in Ottawa and I am currently a student of computer programming at Algonquin College. I've found this program to be wonderfully engaging and after many years spent as a career bartender I am happy for the change of pace. It's exciting to learn a skillset that might scratch my creative itch.

Apart from my studies I am a maker, a musician, and a visual artist. My art is what I will be showcasing with this website.
